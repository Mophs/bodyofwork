“And another thing, about... borrowing clouds…” were the last words whispered, in a time before we turned our eyes up to the sky.

Long before we had seized the golden steps to the fountain from which we then drank, there was a time otherwise. The cosmos flowing, backwards, into a pinhole the size of the deep yet narrow well here. So long since inversion.
